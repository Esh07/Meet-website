My Work

Picture of Child

This photograph captures a moment of childlike wonder and curiosity. The soft focus of the background creates a dreamy, ethereal atmosphere, while the sharpness and detail of the subject draws the viewer in. The child's expression and body language convey a sense of innocence and playfulness, inviting us to join them in their world of imagination and discovery.

Hotel Taj

This photograph captures a mesmerizing scene of a flock of pigeons in flight. Against the backdrop of the stunning Taj Hotel and the bright daylight, the blur of the pigeons' wings and the sharp focus on their bodies create a sense of movement and energy. The composition of the image is expertly crafted, drawing the viewer's eye towards the center where the pigeons are most active. The interplay of light and shadow, as well as the juxtaposition of the natural and man-made elements, make this photograph a true work of art.

Thor's Cave

I had the opportunity to capture a stunning photograph of Thor's Cave during a recent excursion. The natural beauty of the area is truly breathtaking, with majestic mountains towering over lush greenery and illuminated by the warm glow of daylight. As I framed the shot, I couldn't help but feel a sense of awe at the magnificence of the scene before me. This photograph is a testament to the power and beauty of nature, and I am proud to share it with others.
